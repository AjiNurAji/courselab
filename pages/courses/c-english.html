English Language

If you want to have a career, English is one of the skills that you inevitably have to have well. The reason is that competition for jobs and careers today is increasingly global.

The good news is, learning English is actually not as difficult as you imagine. You can even learn it yourself with some of the following self-taught English learning materials.

The first basic material in learning English is learning vocabulary. Mastering vocabulary will indirectly improve your English language skills.

Adding vocabulary means we have to memorize and understand at the same time. Of course, start with simple things first. Starting from recognizing numbers, letters, names of days, months, then moving on to daily activities.

The next material we will study is grammar. Don't immediately imagine complicated material. Grammar in Indonesian is similar to SPOK (Subject, Predicate, Object and Description).

Grammar is rules about word structure to form perfect sentences so that they are clear and easy to understand.

Some parts of grammar include the following:

  • Pronoun

  • Noun

  • Verb

  • Adjective

  • Adverb

  • Determiner

  • Preposition

  • Conjunction

  • Pronoun

    A pronoun or pronoun is a word that is used to replace a noun, whether in the form of the name of a person, animal, object or place. An example is she, he for pronouns that indicate whether he is a woman or a man. Then it, that, this are used as pronouns for animals, objects and also places.

  • Auxiliary verb (to be)

    Tobe is a verb that consists of is, am, are, each of which is paired with a subject, namely I, you, we, they, she, he, it. As a beginner who is definitely trying to put together sentences with auxiliary words, this is very important for you to understand. Tobe for I is am, then (you, we, they) use to be are, and (she, he, it) use tobe is.

  • Tenses

    Turning to the tenses material, there are 16 tenses that you need to master to be truly proficient in English. But as a beginner, there are 3 tenses that you must understand and master, namely:

    • Simple present tense

      You must understand the simple present tense because it is the most basic form of English. If described in a simple formula, the present tense has a form like this: Subject + Verb1. The resulting sentence is the simplest sentence but is very common and widely used.

    • Present continuous tense

      The function of the present continuous tense is to form English sentences that state events that are taking place. The explanation in formula form is Subject + To be + Verb1-Ing. Mastering the present continuous will be very helpful in practicing when having daily conversations.

    • Simple past tense

      Having a function to express past or ongoing activities, the simple past tense is also mandatory for you to master. The form of the simple past tense is Subject + Verb2.

Pronunciation or pronunciation should not be ignored when learning English. Pronunciation errors should be avoided to improve overall word pronunciation skills.

Pronunciation is a part of speaking. In learning how to pronounce in English, Listening Skills or our ability to hear vocabulary are also needed.
